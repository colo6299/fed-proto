Online learning, reimagined.

Federate is changing the shape of online learning by matching you with other users interested in the same topics.

Group support

When learning online, it can help to work through topics as part of a group. Whether it's learning a language, or learning to juggle—it's easy to keep your momentum when you have people to give you a push. Federate will help match you with people you can learn well with.

Agile learning

Maintain the flexibility of online learning, while gaining the support structure of a peer group. Maintain any number of topic groups with people at your skill level in that domain.

Community mentorship

Learning teams can match with topic mentors, who help to 'unblock' team members and help to build collaborative sills in the team. Any community member can volunteer as a mentor if they have the experience and teamwork skills.
